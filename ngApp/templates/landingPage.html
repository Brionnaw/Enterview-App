About

ENTERVIEW platform helps gather public information about employers to help you on your big day. ENTERVIEW was design to be an easy one stop shop to gather information about a company and brush up on common questions and answers before your big day. ENTERVIEW connects with several APIs to gather as much public data as possible on companies to help imform the interviewee before a conversation. An Interview should naturally gravitate toward a conversation for you and the interviewee to feel comfortable. A way to make this happen is to know as much about the company as possible to have the conversation starters. ENTERVIEW help solve that problem and leaves a good impression to employers.
